ABOUT US

Welcome to EcoShift, your trusted partner in hospital waste management. Our mission is to provide healthcare facilities with the tools and resources they need to manage their waste safely, efficiently, and in compliance with environmental and health regulations. At EcoShift, we are dedicated to transforming how hospitals handle their waste. With the increasing amount of medical waste generated daily, it is crucial to have a reliable system in place to ensure proper segregation, identification, and disposal. Our platform is designed to meet these needs, making waste management simpler and more effective for hospitals across Nepal.
